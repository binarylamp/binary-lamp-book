# Binary Lamp Style Guide

## Mission

Making IT and English accessible to non-engineers.

Building a book in public.

## Audience

- Non-engineers
- IT beginners
- Students
- Professionals learning IT English

## Writing Style

- Teach ideas, not just vocabulary.
- Write mainly in natural English.
- Start with a real-world situation, not a dictionary definition.
- Explain the original meaning before the IT meaning.
- Make each article feel like a short essay, not a glossary entry.
- Preserve the original news context and source URL.
- Add Japanese notes at the end.

## Source News

- Original article title
- Publisher
- URL
- Publication date
- Why this article was selected

## Workflow

News
→ Select one word
→ Create a draft
→ Human review
→ Publish

## Goal

Build a practical IT English book through continuous public writing on GitHub.
